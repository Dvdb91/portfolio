Hi, I‘m Olivia, a graphic designer from Switzerland

My longing desire to design stems from my affinity for order and geometry, as well as my strong visual and spacial thinking. Due to my attention to detail, I always look for inspiration outside of design and I strive to bring a sense of simplicity, clarity, authenticity and appropriateness to every project. I am very passionate about my work and driven to satisfy my clients.

I’m raised by the amazing Blitz&Donner team, starting as an intern, working part time as a graphic designer during my studies and working full time with them, after successfully completing my Bachelor of Arts in Visual Communication. Within those exciting five years, I began to understand the importance of working interdisciplinarily on numerous channels and learned the beauty of clear and solid communication. I developed a belief in design that is more than just a empty wrap, but understands the clients best interests and represents them accordingly.

I have the need to constantly evolve and advance my skill set, and alongside my main discipline of print design and branding, I have done work in photography, illustration, animation, web design, typography, video-production and texting. I believe in working hard, sitting up straight, embracing the unexpected and being punctual, but most of all I believe in the power of open, honest communication, fostering relationships and having a bit of fun along the way. At the end of the day, I just want to make great things with great people.

I am currently based in Egypt where I work and live with my favourite Belgian person. In my spare time I am a Dive Instructor, and if I had a superpower, it would be breathing underwater. I love loud music and literally reducing my home to a backback from time to time. Brunch is definitely my favourite meal of the day – with a book or friends by my side.

Right now, I’m looking for new projects and cooperations. Want to have a coffee? Cool! Contact me.
